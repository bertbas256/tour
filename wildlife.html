Maanlig's Extraordinary Wildlife

Maanlig Safaris is home to an incredible diversity of wildlife, from majestic big cats to rare bird species. Our conservation efforts have created a sanctuary where animals thrive in their natural habitats.

With over 200 species of mammals and 400 species of birds, Maanlig offers unparalleled wildlife viewing opportunities throughout the year.
